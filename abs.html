Leg Raises


Leg raises are a bodyweight exercise that targets the abdominal muscles and hip flexors. Lie flat on your back and keep your legs straight. Lift your legs off the ground by engaging your lower abs, then lower them back down without letting your feet touch the ground.






Plank


The plank is a simple yet effective core exercise. Begin in a push-up position, resting on your forearms instead of hands. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels, engaging your core, glutes, and shoulders. Hold this position for a set amount of time.






Hollow Body


The hollow body is an exercise that involves lying on your back while engaging your core muscles to create a flat, extended body position. Lift your legs and shoulders slightly off the ground, while keeping your lower back pressed into the floor. Your arms can also be extended overhead. The hollow body position builds core strength, enhances stability, and improves body awareness.
