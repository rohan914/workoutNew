Preacher curls


Preacher curls are an isolation exercise targeting the biceps. Perform them using a preacher curl bench and an EZ-curl bar or dumbbells. Sit with your upper arms resting on the bench's pad and grip the barbell or dumbbells with an underhand grip. Curl the weight towards your shoulders while exhaling, keeping your upper arms stationary. Squeeze your biceps at the top and slowly lower the weight back down.
